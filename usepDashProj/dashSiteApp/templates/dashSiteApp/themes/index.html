University Dashboard

The University of Southeastern Philippines (USeP) Dashboard was intended for monitoring and evaluation of the actual operations of the university.

These include information about enrollment, students, faculty and staff, and the University's physical and financial status.

The University dashboard is categorized into three areas: Operational Dashboard, Strategic Dashboard, and Analytical Dashboard.
